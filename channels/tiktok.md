# TikTok AI Assistant

Purpose:

Connect AstraMind with TikTok content workflows.

Features:

- Content ideas
- Video script creation
- Caption generation
- Hashtag suggestions
- Audience analysis
- Content planning

Capabilities:

- Create short video concepts
- Generate creative ideas
- Help creators organize content
- Prepare publishing plans

Rules:

- Follow platform guidelines
- Avoid harmful content
- Respect creators and users

Goal:

Provide an AI assistant for TikTok creators.
